Pavithra is a dedicated Psychologist with 1 year of hands-on counselling experience, helping students and young professionals manage stress, anxiety, and relationship challenges. She completed her UG in Psychology at SRM University, Chennai campus, and earned her M.Sc. in Psychology from GITAM University, Andhra campus. Rooted in a client-centred, empathetic approach, she blends evidence-based techniques with warm, non-judgmental listening to create a safe space for growth. Pavithra focuses on emotional well-being, resilience building, and personal development, and stays current with new therapeutic practices and research. Her goal is to make mental-health support accessible and foster lasting positive change in the lives she touches. She also believes in the power of open communication to heal and transform lives. Pavithra has been part of workshops and awareness programs to promote mental well-being among youth. She emphasizes holistic growth by balancing emotional, cognitive, and social aspects of health. Her compassionate nature and professionalism make her approachable to individuals from diverse backgrounds. With a vision to normalize mental health conversations in society, Pavithra continues to inspire confidence and hope in her clients.
